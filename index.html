Who we are

RIFA - Charity Events

Roosevelt Island Football Association believes in giving back to the community. We have held many charity tournaments throughout the years, that have collected thousands of dollars for a multitude of causes throughout the world.

Past Events

Ecuador Earthquake Relief Tournament

A deadly 7.8 magnitude earthquake hit the coast of Ecuador in April of 2016. Roosevelt Island Soccer held a one day tournament to collect funds to help those in need. We were able to send over 1K to buy essential items with the help of a local nonprofit organizations.

2018 RIFA World Cup Charity Tournament

As a celebration of the 2018 World Cup, the Roosevelt Island Football Association held tournament that collected over 2k in funds fot The Treatment Action Campaign (TAC). TAC is an organizationthat works to provide access to quality public healthcare in South Afrida since 1998.

2016 RIFA Charity Tournament

In the summer of 2016, we celebrated a tournament that collected over 1k in funds. We shared those funds with the Malaria Foundation, Ball4All, and South Bronx United.

2014 RIFA World Cup Tournament

As a celebration of the 2014 World Cup, the Roosevelt Island Football Association held tournament that saw Team Americas collect the cup. In the final, Team America defeated Team Europe 3-2 in a thrilling game.

About Roosevelt Island Football Association

Created 10 years ago, we are a group of soccer players from all over the world that gather togethere in the football fields of Roosvelt Island to play the game we love. We play all year round and we hold charity tournamets twice a year. If you are interested in joining our group, drop us a line via email or follow us through Instagram.
