About Roosevelt Island Football Association

Created 10 years ago, we are a group of soccer players from all over the world that gather togethere in the football fields of Roosvelt Island to play the game we love. We play all year round and we hold charity tournamets twice a year. If you are interested in joining our group, drop us a line via email or follow us through Instagram.
